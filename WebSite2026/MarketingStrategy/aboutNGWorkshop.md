# NG Workshop - Company Profile

## Company Overview

**NG Workshop** is an Israeli technology consulting and software development company specializing in integrating AI agents into business processes. As the **official Camunda Partners in Israel**, we bring a unique approach to AI adoption: embedding AI within documented, controlled, and transparent business processes.

## Mission Statement

> To help Israeli enterprises adopt AI safely and effectively by integrating AI agents into well-defined, monitored business processes - not as standalone black boxes, but as transparent, controllable components within existing workflows.

## Unique Value Proposition

NG Workshop differentiates itself through a **Process-First AI** approach:

1. **Transparency** - Every AI decision is documented and traceable
2. **Control** - Human-in-the-Loop at critical decision points
3. **Compliance** - Full audit trails for regulatory requirements
4. **Stability** - AI embedded in proven process orchestration platform
