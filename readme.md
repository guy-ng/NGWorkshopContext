## Mission & Positioning

**Tagline:** "Embedding AI Agents in Business Processes - Safely and Under Full Control"

**Core Message:** "AI that works within the process, not instead of it"

NG Workshop specializes in the smart integration of AI agents within business processes using the world-leading Camunda BPM platform. This integration enables intelligent automation, faster decision-making, and significant efficiency gains - while maintaining full control and transparency.
